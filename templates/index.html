Voice Analytics Agent

Ask your data questions out loud and get spoken business insights back.

This prototype listens through the browser, translates natural language into secure SQL, analyzes an internal SQLite dataset, and answers with concise insight summaries instead of raw rows alone.

Try: "Which region has the highest revenue?" or "Show the monthly profit trend for Alpha."

Security model Read-only, schema-limited SQL
Conversation mode Follow-up context memory
Insight focus Trends, anomalies, and risks
