# Sample Client Questions

Use these during demos, testing, or requirement gathering.

## Revenue And Sales

- Which region has the highest revenue?
- Show the monthly revenue trend for Alpha.
- Which product line is performing best in April?
- What about the South region?

## Cost And Profitability

- Which region has the lowest profit?
- Show the monthly profit trend for Beta.
- Where are costs rising fastest?

## Service And Risk

- What are the biggest risk hotspots?
- Which area has the most incidents?
- Show customer churn by region.
- Which product has the lowest customer satisfaction?

## Follow-Up Style Questions

- What about North?
- Show the same for Beta.
- And in March?
- Which one is worst?
